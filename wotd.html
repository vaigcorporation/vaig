QUINTESSENCE

quintessence: (n.) 1. the fifth and highest element after air and earth and fire and water; was believed to be the substance composing all heavenly bodies; ether;
2. the purest and most concentrated essence of something;
3. the most typical example or representative of a type; (v.) to distil or extract as a quintessence; to reduce to a quintessence. “Truth quintessenced and raised to the highest power. - J. A. Symonds.” .
